Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter (melted)

Instructions

  1. Mix the warm milk with the yeast and sugar in a bowl. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add the egg, egg yolk, and melted butter to the yeast mixture.
  3. Gradually add the bread flour, mixing until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 2 hours or until doubled in size.
  6. After rising, roll out the dough into a large rectangle.
  7. Mix the melted butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon for the filling. Spread this mixture evenly over the rolled-out dough.
  8. Roll the dough tightly into a Swiss roll shape and cut into 3cm sections.
  9. Place the rolls flat in a greased baking tray.
  10. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and bake for 20-25 minutes until lightly golden brown.
  11. Let the rolls cool slightly before serving.
